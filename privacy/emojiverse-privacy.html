Privacy Policy for EmojiVerse

Last updated: June 6, 2026

EmojiVerse ("we", "our", or "us") is a free mobile puzzle game. This policy explains what data is collected when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. However, the following third-party services are used:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app (e.g. which levels are played, button taps). No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Crashlytics to detect and fix crashes. It collects crash logs and device information. No personal data is included. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Game progress (level, lives) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

EmojiVerse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
